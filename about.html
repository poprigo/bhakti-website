Why 'Bhakti'?

In ancient Sanskrit, Bhakti translates to devotion, participation, and love. It is the surrender of the self to something greater.

We chose this name because we believe celebration is a form of worship. Whether it is a corporate gala or a retail launch, transforming a space is an act of intention. It is a way of declaring, "This moment matters."

The Humility of Paper

In a world obsessed with heavy plastics, we chose paper. Because paper is alive. It breathes. It has grain, texture, and structural integrity.

Our decor is engineered to withstand the rigorous logistics of global import, yet it remains fundamentally organic—designed to captivate environments with zero environmental guilt.

From Porbandar to Surat

Our story begins in the coastal quiet of Amar, Porbandar, and finds its industrial heartbeat in Surat. Founded by Ankit Detroja, Bhakti Decor is deeply rooted in Gujarat’s legendary history of trade and craftsmanship.

We brought together generations of artisanal knowledge and paired it with state-of-the-art manufacturing precision to dominate the global market.

Engineered for Global Import

We do not just create art; we engineer supply chains. We understand the precise demands of international distributors and large-scale retailers.

Our manufacturing processes are strictly optimized for high-volume wholesale, ensuring every unit maintains flawless structural consistency upon arrival.

We are the architects of joy. We manufacture the silence before the applause, and the color behind the laughter. We are Bhakti Decor.
Ankit Detroja, Founder
