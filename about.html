01. The Name

Why 'Bhakti'?

In ancient Sanskrit, Bhakti translates to devotion, participation, and love. It is the surrender of the self to something greater.

We chose this name because we believe celebration is a form of worship. Whether it is a wedding, a birth, or a festival, the act of decorating a space is an act of love. It is a way of saying, "This moment matters."

We do not just manufacture decor; we craft the instruments of your devotion.

02. The Medium

The Humility of Paper

In a world obsessed with permanence and plastic, we chose paper. Why? Because paper is alive. It breathes. It has grain, texture, and memory.

There is a beautiful fragility to paper decor. It is not meant to last forever—it is meant to be perfect for now. This impermanence makes it precious. Like a flower that blooms for a day, our creations are designed to captivate you in the moment.

"We are the architects of joy. We construct the silence before the applause, and the color behind the laughter. We are Bhakti Decor."
